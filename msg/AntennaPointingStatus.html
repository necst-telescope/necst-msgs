necst_msgs.msg.AntennaPointingStatus Reference

necst_msgs.msg.AntennaPointingStatus

Consolidated antenna pointing/tracking status for progress/diagnostics.

Coordinates are low-level apparent Alt-Az values in degrees. Derived RA/Dec or Galactic display coordinates should be calculated by a display/analysis layer using neclib/astropy with the timestamps below.
